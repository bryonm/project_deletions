Ambuya Development Center - Blantyre, Malawi

In Tanganyika Village, many children endure the sharp pains of hunger. An army worm infestation has devastated the maize crops that villagers rely on for nutrition, making matters even worse for impoverished families this year. Even so, hungry children struggle to attend school and earn their way out of poverty.

The Ambuya Development Center ministers to students and strives to provide lunches for little ones in need. Your generous gift will support this incredible ministry as they nourish both bodies and souls. Donate now to share your blessings with poor children in Malawi!
