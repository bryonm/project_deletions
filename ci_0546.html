New World Christian School - Valle de los Sillos, Ecuador

You can provide a Christian education and vocational training for at-risk youth in Ecuador who have been forced by poverty to work in the city streets.

A gift to Cross International will help cover teachers’ salaries to educate students who attend night classes at New World Christian School, a program tailored to meet the particular needs of poor, working youth. With your support, the school will put Bibles in the hands of the poor and give them the ability to read God’s words on the pages!

Read the PDF to Learn More

 

We meticulously honor the intent of our donors. 100% of all proceeds are restricted for the specific project for which they are raised. In the rare event that more funds are received than are required for a specific project, the additional funds will be used to meet the most urgent needs of the poor.

All donations made to Cross International are tax deductible and receipts will be provided. If you choose to contribute without seeking additional information, please remember to include the project name and number with your gift. Thank you and may God bless you!

For more information, e-mail us at info@crossinternational.org and include the project number in the correspondence. Thank you!
