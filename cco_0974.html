A Favor del Niño (In Favor of the Child) – 0974 – Mexico City, Mexico

Category: Food

In Mexico City, compassionate Catholic lay leaders operate A Favor del Niño (In Favor of the Child) to provide poor children with educational opportunities and the hope of a better future. Not only do these children receive a quality Catholic education, but the school provides three meals a day plus snacks to ease the burden on their poor, but hardworking parents.
