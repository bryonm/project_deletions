Kobonal Mission Water Project - 0770 - Kobonal, Haiti

Category: Water

The people living in Haiti’s Central Plateau region face extreme poverty. Before the Kobonal Haiti Catholic Mission was established, the people lived in dilapidated dirt huts, they had little access to jobs, and they struggled to even feed themselves. The communities in the region have been transformed by the love of Christ and the Mission’s social development programs, but a great need still exist: None of the villagers have access to clean water.
