Namaacha Water Project - 1375 - Namaacha, Mozambique

Category: Water

In the border town of Namaacha, the HIV/AIDS crisis has left a devastating trail of orphans, single-parent Households and street children. These vulnerable children are at risk for trafficking, abuse, teen pregnancy and truancy from school – but the Franciscan Missionaries of Mary are reaching out to them through a day care and afterschool program that meets the children’s nutritional, educational and spiritual needs. Unfortunately, what this important outreach lacks is clean, safe water. For years, due to an extended drought and inadequate facilities, the center has suffered because there is no safe running water for the children to drink. Your gift will allow the Namaacha Children’s Center to drill a new deep-water well.
