Pwoje Espwa Orphans - 0112 - Les Cayes, Haiti

Category: Orphans and Vulnerable Children

Hungry, homeless and without hope – those words should never be used to describe a child. But sadly, this is the reality for thousands of poor children living in Haiti. That’s why Cross Catholic Outreach supports Pwoje Espwa (“Project Hope”), a Catholic home that provides a loving environment and lasting hope to some of Haiti’s most desperate children. Founded by Father Marc Boisvert, Espwa offers shelter, food, education and spiritual nourishment to more than 450 orphaned and destitute boys and girls. Your gift can give a child hope!
