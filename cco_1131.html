Amigos for Christ Clean Kitchens - 1131 - Chinandega, Nicaragua

Category: Housing

This project gives kitchens to poor families who prepare meals on unsanitary surfaces and cook on rudimentary stoves that fill their homes with toxic smoke. With the help of Cross, families are educated about clean air kitchens and learn to maintain a safe, hygienic and enjoyable place to cook. Click here for more info or donate now below.
