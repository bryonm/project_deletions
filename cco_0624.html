0624 - Rainbow House - Port-au-Prince, Haiti

Category: Orphans & Vulnerable Children

Many Haitian children who have been orphaned or infected with HIV/AIDS are stigmatized by their society and treated like second-class citizens. To address this difficult issue, Catholic lay-missionaries Bob and Danielle Pènette founded Rainbow House in 1995. The orphanage offers these children a loving Christ-centered home, an education, nutrition and the proper medical treatments for their fragile conditions.
