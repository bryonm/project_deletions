Casa De Vida - Guatemala City, Guatemala

Many women and children in Guatemala City suffer unimaginable abuse at the hands of husbands and male relatives. A group of Christian women decided to do something about it by founding Casa de Vida, “House of Life.”

Their ministry provides a safe house where women and children who have escaped domestic violence can recover and establish a new life. They are depending on Cross International and its benefactors to help cover the cost of this life-saving program. Help now!
